The Habitation and Logistics Outpost (HALO)

Also called the Minimal Habitation Module (MHM) and formerly known as the Utilization Module. HALO will be launched by a single Falcon Heavy rocket in Nov. 2024 along with the PPE Module. The module will consist of two axial and up to two radial docking ports, life suppourt systems, control and data handling capabilities, energy storage and energy distrubution, thermal control, and communication and tracking abilities. HALO will have the ability to house a crew of four for atleast 30 days.

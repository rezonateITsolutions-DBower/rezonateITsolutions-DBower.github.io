Orbital Launches

In addition to becoming the primary launch vehicle for SpaceX, the Starship will be used to assist in the deployment of over 12,000 internet satellites, in conjunction with Starlink, over the next 5 years. This is just one of many intended uses for the Starship System other examples could include:

  • Long-duration journeys beyond the earth and moon system and even into the outer planets
  • Sending crew such as space tourist to the ISS, Lunar Gateway, and other orbital stations.
  • The Starship HLS (Human Landing System) will be used to take crew and cargo to the surface and back again.

Lunar Gateway

"The Lunar Gateway, or simply Gateway, is a planned small space station in lunar orbit intended to serve as a solar-powered communications hub,
science laboratory, short-term habitation module for goverment-agency astronauts, as well as a holding area for rovers & other robots."

Planned modules
  • PPE
  • HALO
  • I-HAB
  • ESPRIT
    Proposed Modules
  1. The Gateway Logistics Modules
  2. The Gateway Airlock Module
  3. The Canadarm3
Power and propulsion
  • Four 6 kW BHT-600 Busek Hall-effect thrusters
  • Two 12.5 kW NASA/Aerojet Rocketdyne Advanced Electric Propulsion System (AEPS) Hall-effect thrusters
  • Total engine output: roughly 50 kW

The Lunar Gateway is just the begining of a renewed interest in the planetary & sub-planetary bodies that reside within our own solar system.
It is my hope that one day, together, we can stand at the boundary of perception to achieve a level of self sustainability, diverse adaptability,and emotional clarity fit for a space bearing civilization.
